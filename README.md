# Automation Spectrum Pty Ltd

> Applied AI Platforms for Energy, Operations and Enterprise Intelligence.

Automation Spectrum Pty Ltd is an Australian enterprise AI company based in Melbourne, Australia, focused on building specialised AI platforms that help organisations reduce operational costs, improve decision-making, and automate complex workflows.

The company develops domain-specific AI products across energy, operations, and enterprise intelligence.
